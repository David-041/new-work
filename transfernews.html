MAN CITY TRANSFER NEWS.


Picture of Erling Haaland in a city shirt

Man city sign Erling Haaland on a five year deal to keep him at the club till 2027. He signed from Borrusia Dortmund. All of Europe's top clubs were linked with the player but he chose only to play for his father's former club and has already scored nine goals in the first month at the club including two consecutive hatricks against crystal palace and Nottingham forest.He could probably win the golden boot.

Picture of Gabriel Jesus in an arsenal shirt

Arsenal signed Gabriel Jesus from Manchester City for a fee of £60m.Gabriel Jesus left Man city in search of game time and due to the fact that man city bought Erling Haaland and Julian Alvarez.He currently plays as the no. 9 at Arsenal and already has three goals in the premier league.

Picture of Raheem Sterling in a chelsea shirt

Chelsea signed Raheem Sterling from manchester city for a fee of £45m. Sterling left city for almost the same reason as Gabriel Jesus, he lost his place in the starting eleven due to underperforming in man city and due to the signing of Jack Grealish last summer,Erling Haaland and Julian Alvarez. He didn't like the idea of being benched almost through out the season so he decided to leave.He already has 4 goals in the premier league for chelsea
